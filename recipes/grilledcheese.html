Grilled Cheese

Steps and Ingredients

This is a recipe for your typical grilled cheese sandwich. Simple to do and tasty to eat! Below are the ingredients needed for this recipe and their measurements

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1-2 slices of cheese
  • Butter

Below are the steps, be sure to follow along carefully and also to read the recipe fully once before actually starting

  1. Butter the bread
  2. Place cheese between the bread slices
  3. Grill on a pan until golden
  4. Flip and cook the other side
  5. Serve and enjoy
