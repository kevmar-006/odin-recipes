Scrambled Eggs

Steps and Ingredients

This is a recipe for your typical scrambled eggs. Simple to do and tasty to eat! Below are the ingredients needed for this recipe and their measurements

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Salt & pepper

Below are the steps, be sure to follow along carefully and also to read the recipe fully once before actually starting

  1. Steps
    1. Crack eggs into bowl and whisk
    2. Melt butter into pan on low heat
    3. Pour eggs in and stir gently
    4. Cook until soft and fluffy
    5. Season
    6. Serve
